Bookbinding

  1. Name the different parts of a book

  2. Do the following:
    1. Name the different materials used in bookbinding.
    2. Name some substitute materials used in bookbinding.
    3. Describe the proper steps in preparing paste, and explain their uses.

  3. Name the tools and equipment used in bookbinding, and explain their uses.

  4. Describe at least four (4) different stitches used in bookbinding.

  5. Bind or rebind any of the following:
    1. Sheets of papers
    2. Volume of loose magazines or newspapers
    3. A damaged book
