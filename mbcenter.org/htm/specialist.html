Specialist Ratings

The Specialist Ratings list on the website has been updated as of January 5, 2016. The update is based on list released by BSP official Yasser F. Sarona.

To earn a specialist rating you must:

  1. Select a category you have not previously earned.
  2. Under the category you have selected, select three merit badges that you have not previously earned.
  3. Earn all three merit badges.
