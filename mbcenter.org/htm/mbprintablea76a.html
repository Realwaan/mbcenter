Scholarship

  1. Show proof that you have been in attendance at school (elementary, high school, evening vocational, or night school) for a period of at least one year.

  2. Present evidence from your teacher or principal that during this period - your attendance has been satisfactory, that you have had an average of 82, and you have no grade below 75 in any subject.

  3. Present evidence that you have taken a satisfactory part in the life of your school.

  4. Give evidence of at least five instances where your school training has been of value to you in Scouting and other activities or interest outside of school.

  5. Give evidence that you have made use of educational resources other than those of your school for self-education.

  6. Present evidence that your behavior, leadership, and service have been better than average.
