Advancement Rank

These are the advancement ranks for both the Boy Scout and Senior Scout programs of the Boy Scouts of the Philippines. Boy Scouting is not a pre-requisite for Senior Scouting. These are now based on the revised Passport Advancement Scheme as of 2019 for Boy Scout Program and 2025 for Senior Scout Program.

Learn the basic idea of the Scout Advancement Process.

Eagle Scout Anahaw Award

  1. Bronze Anahaw. Earning 6 additional Merit Badges after earning the Eagle Scout.
  2. Silver Anahaw. Earning 6 additional Merit Badges after earning the Bronze Anahaw.
  3. Gold Anahaw. Earning 6 additional Merit Badges after earning the Silver Anahaw.
