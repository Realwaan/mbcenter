About Us

The Merit Badge Center is the online resource center for the members of the Boy Scouts of the Philippines. It is our purpose to assist the leaders and youth members of this organization in their effort to deliver the Scouting program. We have a compendium of resources coming from various sources with some original works of our own. Included in this collection are links to useful websites that provides information related to rank and merit badge requirements.
