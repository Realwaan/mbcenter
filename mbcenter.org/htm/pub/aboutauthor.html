About the Author

I was introduced to Scouting in 1992 when I joined Senior Outfit 1 of Quezon City Council. Served in multiple position-of-office throughout the years in two National Scout Associations: Patrol Quartermaster, Senior Crew Leader, Assistant Outfit Advisor, Troop Leader, Institutional Advancement Chair, District Executive, Assistant Scoutmaster, Scoutmaster, District Committee Member, Troop Committee Member, and Crew Committee Chairman.
