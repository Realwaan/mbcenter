Advancement Worksheets

We are working to update Merit Badges based on the Updated Senior Scout Passport issued this year (2025).

Use these worksheet to assist you or your Scouts to do their advancement and merit badge work. As of the moment we are just starting this project. Microsoft Word format is provided so you may customize the worksheet with your own branding.

Worksheets for all Merit Badges with requirements are available. We are working on getting worksheets done for the passport Advancement Ranks. After that initiative, we will review existing Merit Badge worksheets to update them. If you encounter issue or have recommendation for changes to the worksheet, please email us at info@meritbadge.org.ph and we will consider your recommendation.
