How to Use this Site

  1. Navigate using the main navigation links found on the left-side by selecting a topic: Advancement Ranks or Merit Badges. Merit Badges are also grouped into Alphabetical Listing, Citizen Required, Eagle Required, and Electives.

  2. Select the badge topic you need to earn or that interests you. Instructions how to earn a Merit Badge are also provided on this website.

  3. Once you have selected a badge you will be presented with the badge requirement on the main content area. The content area will also provide a graphic of the badge. Citizen or Eagle required Merit Badges will have the word "REQUIRED" next to the picture. There will be additional links on the left-side navigation bar. The additional links are categorized as follows:

    1. Resource Links provides third-party websites with information to assist Scouts and Scouters in completing one or more of the requirements for the badge. Third-party websites are websites external to the Merit Badge Center.

    2. Local Links provides on-us web pages or adobe acrobat documents to assist Scouts and Scouters in completing one or more of the requirements for the badge. Under local links are also downloadable Badge Worksheets that will assist you in completing the requirements and make the process a bit organized.

    3. Specialized Links provides third-party websites that are grouped into a specific category or topic, such as "Civic Organizations" or "Scouting Events".

    4. Website Tools will have a link to a printable version of the requirements and an e-mail address to report broken links
