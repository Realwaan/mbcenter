Pathfinder Scout Rank

Senior Scout Ideals

  1. Narrate your life experience wherein you were able to show example of living up to the Scout Oath and Law, the Senior Scout Code, the Scout Motto, Senior Scout Slogan, and Mission and Vision.

  2. Show proof of your spiritual growth and have been faithful to your religious obligations.

  3. Together with your Crew members in your school, volunteer to lead in at least three (3) flag-raising and flag-lowering ceremonies during Philippine Flag Day and/or Scouting Month.

Social Activities

  1. Identify and explain with your Crew members the different functions of the Outfit Program Committees and how they help the Crew in accomplishing their advancement requirements.

  2. Discuss with your friends, peers and fellow Scouts the harmful effects of illegal drugs and substance abuse, including alcoholism and smoking. Explain why young people engage into these activities and how you can help to avoid and prevent it.

  3. Discuss with your peers, friends, and fellow Scouts how teenagers must behave in social parties at home, school and communities with the members of the opposite sex.

  4. Explain how to show courtesy and respect to the rights and properties of others.

  5. Show proof that you are actively participating (Sports Activities) with your Crew and Outfit in outdoor activities that promote a healthy lifestyle.

Vocational Activities

  1. Engage into simple and basic vocational and entrepreneurial activities that will help you earn and save money to help your parents.

  2. Show proof of increase in your "Scout is Thrifty" savings account in your preferred bank or e-wallet provider.

  3. Together with your Crew members, plan, organize, and conduct "Scout's Good Turn" habits through a School or Community. These service projects may be related to Environment, Development, and Peace.

Outdoor and Indoor Activities

  1. Plan, organize and go for a 5-6 kilometers exploration with your Outfit and explain with your fellow Scouts how to find directions with and without using a compass, describe and predict weather and environment conditions, and demonstrate safe exploration procedures.

  2. Earn the following Merit Badges:

    1. Environment

    2. Weather

    3. First Aid

    4. Signalling

    5. Navigation
