Business

  1. Write a satisfactory business letter and a personal letter.

  2. Know simple bookkeeping, shorthand, or typing.

  3. Make a budget of your expenses, including those for clothes and incidentals. Keep a complete and actual account of personal receipts and expenditures for three months.

  4. Be prepared to answer questions and problems on interest, percentages, and discounts.

  5. Obtain and hold a job in a company or business organization for at least one month (after school hours, during weekends, or vacations periods). Present a certificate from your employer that for the period you were employed - you put into practice the Scout Oath and Law, and have shown your efficiency in your attendance, due regard for your general appearance; or render one year successful service as a Troop or Outfit Scribe, under the same conditions.
