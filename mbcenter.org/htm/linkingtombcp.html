Linking to the Merit Badge Center

There are many ways to link to the Merit Badge Center.

Text Link

You may link to meritbadge.org.ph.

Specific Merit Badges

You may link to specific merit badges using the URL:

http://www.mbcenter.org/meritbadges.php?mb=meritbadge

Simply change "meritbadge" with the exact name of the Merit Badge you want to link to in lower case and with no space whatsoever.

To link to Soil and Water Conservation simply link to:

http://www.mbcenter.org/meritbadges.php?mb=soilandwaterconservation
